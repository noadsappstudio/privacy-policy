Summary

Blackjack: Road to Casino is a blackjack training app. We are committed to your privacy. We do not run ads, do not sell your data, and do not track your behavior.

Some features (leaderboard, global rankings) require sending a small amount of aggregated, pseudonymous data to our servers. This is clearly described below and optional.

No Ads

The app contains no advertising. We do not use any advertising SDK or network.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device, using local storage. It is never sent to our servers unless you opt into leaderboard features:

  • Training progression and statistics (accuracy, speed, hands played)
  • Optional player profile: username (pseudonym), country, gender — entered voluntarily by the user
  • App preferences (language, onboarding status)

You can delete this data at any time by clearing app data in your device settings, or using the reset option in the app settings.

Data Sent to Our Servers Leaderboard feature

When the leaderboard feature is enabled (upcoming feature), the following aggregated data is sent to our servers to calculate global rankings:

  • Player ID — a randomly generated anonymous identifier (UUID). It is not linked to your real identity, email, or any personal account.
  • Country — the country you selected in your profile (optional). Used for country-based rankings.
  • Aggregated training statistics — overall accuracy, count gap, bet quality, deviation accuracy, median speed. These are summary numbers, not raw hand-by-hand decisions.
  • Username (pseudonym) — the display name you chose. Not required to be your real name.

This data is not linked to your real identity. Participating in the leaderboard is optional. If you do not set a username or country, you will not appear in any public ranking.

Leaderboard data is refreshed once per day. We do not store your individual hand decisions on our servers.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics service, crash reporting tool, or behavioral tracking SDK. No usage patterns, session data, or navigation events are collected or transmitted.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for commercial purposes. We do not integrate advertising networks, data brokers, or social media SDKs.

Internet Permission

The app requests the INTERNET permission required by the Android WebView framework and for leaderboard data synchronization. No external requests are made during offline use.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at noadsapp.studio@gmail.com. For local data, use the reset option directly in the app.

Children

This app is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.
